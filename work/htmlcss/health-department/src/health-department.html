Coronavirus: lifting of quarantine and home office obligation

Bern, 02.02.2022 – The Federal Council will lift the home office obligation and contact quarantine from tomorrow. The following also apply, among other things: home office recommendation, mask requirement in the workplace and isolation of people who have tested positive for COVID-19.

Coronavirus: Swiss Federal Council extends measures

Bern, 19.1.2022 – Quarantine and homeworking requirement apply until end-February, the other measures until end-March. In addition, the testing regime for entry into Switzerland will change on 22 January and COVID certificates will still be valid for 270 days at the end of January.
